Description

Introducing the new 2021 Polygon Trid ZZ! 2 years of testing and development in collaboration with the best free riders in the world, Polygon has now released their much-anticipated Slope Style bike - the Trid ZZ. The Bike Anthony Messere chooses to take on the world's toughest freeride competitions. The custom-designed ALX alloy frame features an ultra-stiff, 69-degree tapered head tube with a 426mm reach and a low 331mm BB height. A threaded BB, and ultra-low standover means this bike is ready for trickery. The bike comes spec'd with a 100mm RockShox Pike DJ fork and Rockshox Monarch rear shock, SRAM GX DH 7 speed drive train and Shimano hydraulic disc brakes. The shifters come standard on the handlebar, however, can be easily moved to the downtube if needed. Coming in a fresh new paint job, this bike is not only spec'd for performance, but looks awesome too.
